Features
Everything you need to track attendance
Powerful tools that work together seamlessly, designed for organizations of any size.

Real-time Face Recognition

Point your camera at a group and instantly identify every member. Bounding boxes highlight each face with names and confidence scores.

  • Live camera, photo upload, or video
  • Green boxes for members, orange for new faces
  • Unknown faces captured automatically

New Member Detection

When someone unrecognized appears, their face is captured and shown in a dedicated panel for admin verification.

  • Automatic face capture for unknowns
  • Verify, label, or link to existing member
  • Count new vs returning visitors

Rich Dashboard & Analytics

Visualize trends with bar charts, track top attendees, and see daily breakdowns at a glance. Export everything as CSV.

  • 7-day attendance chart
  • Member profiles with photos
  • CSV import & export

Team & Access Control

Invite team members as admins or viewers. Duplicate face prevention ensures no one is registered twice.

  • Role-based permissions
  • No duplicate faces allowed
  • Multi-organization support
